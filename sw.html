Software

List of software artifacts that I have developed during the course of my ongoing research. Follow me on github: https://github.com/ajamshed/

I. Kargus

Brief Description: A highly scalable signature-based intrusion detection system.
Role: Lead maintainer.
Source: Copyrighted to National Security Research Institute (NSRI), South Korea.

II. mTCP

Brief Description: A highly scalable user-level TCP/IPv4 stack for multicore systems.
Role: Co-lead maintainer with Eunyoung Jeong.
Source: https://github.com/mtcp-stack/mtcp ( , , ), Star-History

III. Packet Bricks

Brief Description: A netmap-based packet layer for distributing and filtering traffic.
Role: Lead maintainer.
Source: https://github.com/zeek/packet-bricks/ ( , , )

IV. mOS Networking Stack

Brief Description: A specialized network programming library for stateful middelboxes.
Role: Lead maintainer.
Source: https://github.com/mos-stack/mOS-networking-stack ( , , )

V. OMEC

Brief Description: Open source virtualized mobile core project from ONF.
Role: Co-maintainer.
Source: https://github.com/omec-project/ngic-rtc ( , , )

VI. High Speed IDS Benchmark Tool

Brief Description: A high speed IDS benchmark tool.
Role: Lead maintainer.
